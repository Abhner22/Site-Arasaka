What is Arasaka

Arasaka Corporation is one of the largest and most powerful megacorporations in the world of Cyberpunk. It deals with security, banking, and manufacturing — and exerts influence over almost every nation. Founded in 1915 by Sasai Arasaka, the Arasaka Corporation stands as the leading megacorporation in security, banking, and military technology. With offices across every continent, Arasaka protects not only assets — but the future itself.
